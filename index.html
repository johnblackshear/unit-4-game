Crystal Collector!

You will be given a random target number at the start of the game. There are four Crystals below. At the start of the game, each crystal be given a random value. You will not see this value until you click on one of crystals. Each time you click on a crystal, the value will be added to your score. You are able to continue clicking on the crystals until you either match the targt number, or until you go over. If your score matches the target number, you win! Otherwise, if you go over you loose! Once you win or loose, the game will reset and the values of target number and crystals will change. Good Luck!
